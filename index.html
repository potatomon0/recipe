Curry
Curry is a well loved dish by lots of folks
Ingredients:
  • Curry packets
  • Chicken thighs
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Oil
Steps
  1. Cut the chicken thighs in chunks
  2. Peel and cut the carrot and potatoes
  3. Add some oil to a non stick pot
  4. Cook the carrot in the oil for 7 mins
  5. Add chicken chunks and cook until no raw meat is visible
  6. Add water and potatoes to the pot and wait until it boil
  7. Turn off the stove and add good amount of Curry packets
  8. Wait for 5 mins then stirr until no curry chunks left
  9. Fire up the stove and simmer for another 5 mins
